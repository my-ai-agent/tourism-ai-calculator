The Business Reality: Cost vs Sales Perspective

When considering any business investment, there are two ways to look at the financial impact:

Cost Reduction Perspective

The savings from AI implementation directly improve your bottom line without requiring additional sales.

For Option 2 above: $17,500 annual savings is pure profit improvement.

Sales Generation Perspective

To achieve the same profit improvement through sales alone:

Required Additional Sales = Desired Profit ÷ Gross Profit Margin

For a business with 30% gross profit margin to generate $17,500 in additional profit:

Required Additional Sales = $17,500 ÷ 0.30 = $58,333 in new sales

This means your AI investment of $5,000 that saves $17,500 is equivalent to generating $58,333 in new sales.
